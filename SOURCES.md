## Research Notes

The Nue is primarily known from *The Tale of the Heike*, but appears in various other Heian and medieval Japanese texts. The creature's chimeric nature and association with imperial illness make it a significant example of Japanese supernatural beliefs during the Heian period.

Modern understanding of the Nue comes from:
1. Classical literary sources (primarily Heike Monogatari)
2. Edo period yokai encyclopedias and illustrations
3. Contemporary folkloristic research
4. Cultural anthropology studies of Japanese supernatural beliefs
